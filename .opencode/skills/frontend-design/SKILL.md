# Frontend Design Skill

## Design Philosophy

Inspired by Japanese aesthetics — *wabi-sabi* (beauty in imperfection), *ma* (negative space as an active element), and *shibui* (understated elegance). Every element must earn its place; nothing is ornamental without purpose.

**Core principle:** Less is more. Clarity over decoration, substance over flourish.

---

## Color Palette — Monochrome

| Role          | Color       | Hex       |
|---------------|-------------|-----------|
| Background    | Pure white  | `#FFFFFF` |
| Surface       | Paper off-white | `#F5F5F0` |
| Light gray    | Subtle tone | `#E8E8E3` |
| Mid gray      | Boundary    | `#999999` |
| Dark gray     | Secondary text | `#666666` |
| Charcoal      | Muted accent | `#333333` |
| Ink black     | Primary text | `#1A1A1A` |

No chromatic colors allowed except in approved photography or artwork. Monochrome discipline ensures visual harmony and focus on content.

---

## Typography

- **Primary:** Clean, legible sans-serif (Noto Sans JP, Inter, or similar)
- **Accent:** Optional restrained serif or brush-stroke style for hero headings — used sparingly
- Hierarchy is expressed through weight (300 / 400 / 700) and size, never through color
- Generous line-height (1.6–1.8 for body) to enhance readability and breathing room

---

## Layout & Spacing

- *Ma* — negative space is a deliberate design tool, not empty waste
- Grid-based layouts with controlled asymmetry
- No unnecessary borders, dividers, or boxes — let whitespace separate content
- Padding and margins are generous; content blocks breathe
- Max-width containers to prevent line lengths from exceeding 60–70 characters

---

## UI Components

| Component | Style |
|-----------|-------|
| Buttons   | Flat, borderless or hairline border, uppercase or no label, subtle opacity or lift on hover |
| Inputs    | Border-bottom only (no full box), minimal focus indicator |
| Cards     | No shadows — use spacing and surface color to distinguish layers |
| Icons     | Minimalist line-art, consistent 1–2px stroke, no fills |
| Navigation | Text-only or single hairline separator, spacious |

Micro-interactions are subtle: gentle fades, shifts, or scale changes (0.98–1.02 range). No bouncy or playful animations.

---

## Imagery & Artistic Elements

- **Photography:** High-contrast black-and-white. Embrace texture — wood grain, fabric, paper, light and shadow, water ripples
- **Illustrations:** Sumi-e (ink wash) style or clean geometric line art
- **Negative space is sacred** — imagery occupies only as much room as needed, leaving the rest to breathe
- Hero images may bleed edge-to-edge, but content overlays are minimal (thin type, low opacity)

---

## Inspiration

- Ryokan (traditional Japanese inn) spatial design
- Muji / ISSEY MIYAKE visual identity — quiet, tactile, intentional
- Sumi-e ink painting and shodo calligraphy — economy of stroke
- Tadao Ando architecture — concrete, light, shadow, precision
- Hiroshi Sugimoto photography — time, minimalism, monochrome mastery
